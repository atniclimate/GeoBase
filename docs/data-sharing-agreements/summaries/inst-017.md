## What it is

Tribal-Related Guidance for USGS Authors is cataloged as a `guidance` issued by U.S. Geological Survey. It covers: USGS authors preparing science information products, including data and software, that relate to Tribal activities, collaborating Tribes, Tribal lands, or Indigenous place names.

The cataloged data scope is: USGS information products, data and software, Tribal names and identifiers, project-progress information, Indigenous place names, and data shared by Tribes with USGS.
