## What it is

Executive Order 13175 ‚Äî Consultation and Coordination With Indian Tribal Governments is cataloged as a `executive-order` issued by President of the United States. It covers: Executive departments and agencies other than independent regulatory agencies; independent regulatory agencies are encouraged to comply. The order concerns federally recognized Indian tribal governments.

The cataloged data scope is: Policies with tribal implications, Tribal input and consultation communications, tribal summary impact statements, written agency-Tribal communications, waiver applications and decisions, and agency certifications to the Office of Management and Budget.
