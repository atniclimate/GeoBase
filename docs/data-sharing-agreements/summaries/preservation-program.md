## What this is

The catalog classifies this document as **guidance**. Covered parties: Members of the public encountering archaeological or cultural resources, qualified professionals seeking to affect their context, and federal, public, and private organizations receiving Tribal preservation services. Covered data or activity: Cultural heritage, archaeological sites, traditional cultural properties, cultural materials, historic records, intellectual property, artifacts, historic structures, and burial-related cultural resources.
