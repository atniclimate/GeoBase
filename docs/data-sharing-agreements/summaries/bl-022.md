## What it is

SB 841 — Relating to tribal public health data is cataloged as a `statute` issued by Oregon Legislative Assembly. It covers: The Oregon Health Authority, federally recognized Indian tribes in Oregon, and tribal epidemiology centers in Oregon that may enter the authorized agreements.

The cataloged data scope is: Reports and investigations of reportable diseases and tribal data available through Oregon's prescription monitoring program.
