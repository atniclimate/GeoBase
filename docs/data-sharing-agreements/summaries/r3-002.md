## What it is

RCW Chapter 43.376 — Government-to-Government Relationship with Indian Tribes is cataloged as a `statute` issued by Washington State Legislature. It covers: Washington state agencies, their heads, tribal liaisons and employees, the governor and statewide elected officials, and the Washington Office of Indian Affairs in relations with federally recognized Indian tribes whose traditional lands and territories included parts of Washington.

The cataloged data scope is: Policies, agreements, program implementation, consultation communications, agency activity reports, liaison contact information, training materials, and Office of Indian Affairs public reports concerning state-Tribal relations.
