## What this is

The catalog classifies this document as **research-code**. Covered parties: Individuals, corporations, agencies, and institutions conducting unrequested or uncontracted studies, surveys, or research on the Colville Indian Reservation; permit holders; and Tribal employees who control Tribal files and records. Covered data or activity: Research designs and applications; funding, personnel, locations, methodologies, intended products and uses; publications and official Tribal comments; interim, progress, and final reports; and information in Tribal files and records.
