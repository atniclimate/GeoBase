## What this is

The catalog classifies this document as **research-code**. Covered parties: All persons subject to the Tribe's civil jurisdiction who undertake research directly affecting the Tribe or Tribal members, and all researchers conducting projects on Tribal lands or directly affecting the Tribe, Tribal lands, or Tribal members, including requested, sponsored, independent, Tribal, state, and federal researchers; defined minor projects receive limited exceptions. Covered data or activity: Research project proposals, applications, contracts, permits, progress and result reports, all project data and work product, documents, correspondence, specimens, raw materials, Tribal Intellectual Property, and information affecting the Tribe, Tribal lands, or Tribal members.
