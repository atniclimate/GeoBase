## What it is

Indigenous Data Governance Brief is cataloged as a `literature` issued by U.S. Indigenous Data Sovereignty Network. It covers: Individuals and entities involved in creating, collecting, assessing, analyzing, interpreting, managing, disseminating, or reusing data in the United States and its territories, including Tribal leaders, researchers, institutions, funders, governments, and other data actors.

The cataloged data scope is: Indigenous data in any format, including information, specimens, and knowledges held by Indigenous Peoples or non-Indigenous entities that relate to, are about, or may affect Indigenous Peoples collectively or individually.
