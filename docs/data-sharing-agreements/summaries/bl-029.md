## What it is

25 U.S.C. Chapter 18 — Indian Health Care is cataloged as a `statute` issued by United States Congress. It covers: The Secretary of Health and Human Services and Indian Health Service; Service-area epidemiology centers; Indian tribes, tribal organizations, urban Indian organizations, and Indian and urban Indian health programs; patients; and authorized recipients of covered records.

The cataloged data scope is: Indian health epidemiological data and protected health information, automated management and patient-care information, behavioral-health plans and outcomes, medical quality-assurance records, and prescription-drug monitoring information.
