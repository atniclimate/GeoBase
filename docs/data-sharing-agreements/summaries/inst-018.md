## What it is

Survey Manual 500.6 — Relations with American Indian and Alaska Native Tribes, Alaska Native Corporations, and Native Hawaiian Organizations is cataloged as a `agency-policy` issued by U.S. Geological Survey. It covers: USGS offices, employees, managers, principal investigators, contractors, collaborators, and non-federal partners engaging with federally recognized Tribes; specified provisions also apply to Alaska Native Corporations and Native Hawaiian Organizations subject to the chapter's stated distinctions.

The cataloged data scope is: Information provided by Tribes or collected by USGS concerning Indigenous Knowledge, cultural resources, culturally significant sites and their locations or attributes, Tribe-owned data, sensitive Tribal data, FOIA-responsive records, science products, contracts, and data-sharing agreements.
