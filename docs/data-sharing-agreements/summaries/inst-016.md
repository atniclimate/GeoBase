## What it is

Exchange Network FAQ — Tribal concerns about grant data-sharing requirements is cataloged as a `guidance` issued by U.S. Environmental Protection Agency. It covers: Tribal applicants and recipients under the EPA Exchange Network Grant Program and EPA grant administrators.

The cataloged data scope is: Proposed project details, summary goals and outputs, developed or reused technology, semiannual progress updates, final reports, and any underlying Tribal environmental or program data that a Tribe elects to share or withhold.

## Cataloged obligations

| Claim ID | Category | Modal | Restated claim | Conditions / exceptions | Cite |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| `inst-016:c001` | `redisclosure-limits` | `descriptive` | The Exchange Network Grant Program currently imposes no obligation on a Tribe to share its actual data outside the Tribe. | The FAQ distinguishes underlying data from required grant summaries and reports; other grant programs, such as Clean Water Act § 106 funding, may have different requirements. | FAQ answer ¶¶ 1-2 |
| `inst-016:c002` | `other` | `must-shall` | At project closeout, a Tribal grant recipient must submit the IT Component and Project Registration form with summary information about accomplished goals and outputs and technology developed or reused, but need not share actual data or specific code. | Applies to the Exchange Network Grant Program closeout form; the obligation is limited to summary information and does not extend to underlying data or specific code. | FAQ answer ¶ 1 |
| `inst-016:c003` | `other` | `must-shall` | A Tribal applicant or recipient must provide project details in application documents, progress updates in semiannual reports, and a final report, while remaining free from an Exchange Network obligation to flow actual data outside the Tribe. | The proposed project must support at least one Exchange Network funding area and meet the program's other requirements; the Tribe may explain its no-external-sharing policy in the application. | FAQ answer ¶ 2 |
| `inst-016:c004` | `ownership-control` | `may-permissive` | A Tribe that chooses or is otherwise required to share some information outside the Tribe may determine which data to share and which data to withhold from external disclosure. | The FAQ presents this discretion even where some sharing occurs for another required grant program or because an exchange is mutually beneficial; it does not define limits imposed by those other programs. | FAQ answer ¶ 3 |
