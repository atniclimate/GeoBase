## What this is

The catalog classifies this document as **irb-policy**. Covered parties: The Northwest Indian College IRB; researchers and institutions whose research is reviewed by that IRB; and participating Tribes, Tribal colleges and universities, and Tribal-based organizations only when they ask the NWIC IRB to review research or otherwise participate under the policy. Covered data or activity: Research involving or about NWIC faculty, staff, and students, plus research accepted for NWIC IRB review at a Tribe, TCU, or TBO's request; research data, biological and non-biological materials, analyses, presentations, reports, publications, and consent documents.
