## What it is

NOT-OD-22-214 — Responsible Management and Sharing of American Indian/Alaska Native Participant Data is cataloged as a `guidance` issued by National Institutes of Health. It covers: Researchers subject to the NIH Data Management and Sharing Policy who plan or conduct NIH-supported research involving American Indian or Alaska Native Tribal Nations; several best practices may also be useful in other Indigenous research contexts but are not presented as responsive to those contexts.

The cataloged data scope is: Scientific and participant data from NIH-supported research involving AI/AN Tribal Nations, including data-management and sharing plans, secondary-use limitations, informed-consent information, Tribal affiliation and group identifiers, repositories, and related biospecimen discussions.
