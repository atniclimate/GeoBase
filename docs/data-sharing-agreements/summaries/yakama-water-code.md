## What this is

The catalog classifies this document as **tribal-code**. Covered parties: The Roads, Irrigation and Land Committee; Water Code Director and staff; water-use and hydraulic-permit applicants and permittees; well owners and drillers; affected Yakama Nation programs; the Bureau of Indian Affairs and Wapato Irrigation Project; other governmental agencies; and persons requesting public records. Covered data or activity: Water-resource studies and data; permit applications and decisions; applicant and property information; geographic, hydrologic, meter, withdrawal, and diversion data; program comments and investigations; well-construction notices; administrative appeal records; and public versus nonpublic Water Code files.
