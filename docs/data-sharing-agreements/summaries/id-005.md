## What it is

Shoshone-Bannock Privacy Policy is cataloged as a `data-governance-policy` issued by Shoshone-Bannock Tribes. It covers: Operators and visitors of the Shoshone-Bannock Tribes' public website, with contracted third parties covered only by the research-analysis disclosure described by the policy.

The cataloged data scope is: Website access telemetry, cookies, web logs, user-submitted personal information, and personal information made available through third-party social-media sites.
