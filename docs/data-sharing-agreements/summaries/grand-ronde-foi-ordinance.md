## What this is

The catalog classifies this document as **tribal-code**. Covered parties: Tribal Administration, the Records Administrator, Tribal members, Tribal newspaper staff acting officially, Tribal officials and employees, and any person who possesses or distributes a Tribal record or information from one. Covered data or activity: Records made, received, filed, or recorded in connection with Tribal business, subject to stated exclusions and including confidential, personal, audit, security, land, privileged, strategy, investment, and mixed exempt/nonexempt records.
