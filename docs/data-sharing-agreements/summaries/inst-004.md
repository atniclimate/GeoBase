## What it is

Tribal Data Sovereignty and the Critical Role of Data in Public Health Governance is cataloged as a `presentation` issued by American Indian Health Commission for Washington State. It covers: Tribal Nations, state and federal public-health jurisdictions, and parties considering the Washington health data-sharing agreement provisions excerpted by the presentation; the slides do not themselves execute or enact those provisions.

The cataloged data scope is: Tribal and AI/AN public-health data, including data held in state, federal, and other datasets, aggregated Tribal data, COVID-19 surveillance and immunization information, and other public-health systems addressed by the model provisions.
