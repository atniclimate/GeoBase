## What it is

Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program (LIHEAP) Agreement is cataloged as a `data-sharing-agreement` issued by Kalispel Tribe of Indians and Washington State Department of Commerce. It covers: Only the Kalispel Tribe of Indians and the Washington State Department of Commerce, as the two parties to the executed agreement.

The cataloged data scope is: Household-member names and Social Security numbers, assistance amounts for people moving into or out of Tribal service territory, and applicant-household referrals, only for preventing duplicate LIHEAP payments when necessary.
