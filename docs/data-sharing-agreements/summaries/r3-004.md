## What it is

25 U.S.C. Chapter 46 — Indian Self-Determination and Education Assistance is cataloged as a `statute` issued by United States Congress. It covers: The Secretaries of the Interior and Health and Human Services, the Comptroller General, recipients of federal financial assistance, Indian tribes, Tribal organizations, and contractors operating under Indian self-determination or self-governance agreements.

The cataloged data scope is: Financial-assistance records, financial statements, audits, program reports, contract and self-governance records, staffing and funding reports, health-status and service-delivery data, funding-agreement information, and Tribal comments on federal reports.
