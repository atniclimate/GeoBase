## What this is

The catalog classifies this document as **tribal-code**. Covered parties: Enrollment applicants and members; the Bureau of Indian Affairs; NPTEC and enrollment staff; Gaming Commission members and staff, gaming operations, employees, license applicants and licensees; fuel, tobacco, liquor, and retail enterprises; the Tribal Athletic Commission; hemp applicants, producers, licensees, laboratories, the Agriculture Office, USDA, and law-enforcement bodies. Covered data or activity: Enrollment records and supporting vital or family documents; gaming licensing, background, financial, audit, and personnel records; business and tax transaction reports; public meeting reports and rules; hemp contact, land, geospatial, production, disposal, testing, enforcement, and transfer records.
