## What it is

RCW Chapter 70.02 — Medical Records — Health Care Information Access and Disclosure is cataloged as a `statute` issued by Washington State Legislature. It covers: Washington health care providers and facilities, mental health service agencies, patients, Indian health care providers, tribal courts and prosecutors, tribal law-enforcement and public-health officers, and personnel of tribal jails, detention facilities, and other authorized recipients.

The cataloged data scope is: Identifiable patient health-care information and records, especially adult and minor mental-health admission, treatment, prognosis, medication, behavior, commitment, discharge, location, public-safety, and care-coordination information shared with or by Tribal health and justice actors.
