## What this is

The catalog classifies this document as **tribal-code**. Covered parties: Persons collecting fish or wildlife data; Tribal members and fish buyers; ceremonial permit holders; guides and outfitters; the Fish and Wildlife Commission; Conservation Enforcement; the Water Resources Division and Commission; water-rights applicants and permittees; and specified state, federal, and Tribal agencies. Covered data or activity: Fish and wildlife samples and scientific data; catch, purchase, ceremonial, guiding, and outfitting reports; archaeological-site information; water-use and permit data; water-rights geodatabases; public permit notices; hearing records; and administrative appeal records.
