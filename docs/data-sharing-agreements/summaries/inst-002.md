## What it is

Data Sovereignty — Free, Prior, and Informed Consent is cataloged as a `data-governance-policy` issued by Tribal Resilience Action Database. It covers: The Tribal Resilience Action Database project team and Tribal Nations, organizations, and communities whose publicly posted climate adaptation, resilience, or managed-retreat plans or derived resilience actions may be included in the database.

The cataloged data scope is: Knowledge and data created and owned by Tribes, including resilience actions derived from public plans, information selected for sharing, use and ownership terms, and information subject to opt-out and removal.
