# Review Boards, Permits, and Oversight

> **DRAFT — NOT NATION-CONFIRMED.** This draft restates merged-catalog claims; no Nation-attributed claim is confirmed by the Nation. Human ratification remains required before presenting any entry as a Nation's requirement.

## Scope and range

This page scans the entire merged catalog (**64 records / 359 claims**) for research review boards, permits, and oversight processes. It includes **32** matching claims: **20** attached to Nation-linked instruments and **12** from intertribal, federal, state, academic, NGO, standards, or other issuers. Modal counts in this slice: `descriptive` 3; `may-permissive` 2; `must-shall` 22; `should-recommended` 5.

Selection rule: catalog category `review-board`. No uncataloged requirement statement has been added.

A modal never overrides legal status: guidance, templates, drafts, presentations, and unknown-status material remain non-binding or unresolved even when their internal wording uses “must.” Conditions and exceptions are part of each restatement.
