# Consent and FPIC

> **DRAFT — NOT NATION-CONFIRMED.** This draft restates merged-catalog claims; no Nation-attributed claim is confirmed by the Nation. Human ratification remains required before presenting any entry as a Nation's requirement.

## Scope and range

This page scans the entire merged catalog (**64 records / 359 claims**) for free, prior and informed consent duties. It includes **39** matching claims: **10** attached to Nation-linked instruments and **29** from intertribal, federal, state, academic, NGO, standards, or other issuers. Modal counts in this slice: `may-permissive` 3; `must-shall` 27; `should-recommended` 9.

Selection rule: catalog category `consent-fpic`. No uncataloged requirement statement has been added.

A modal never overrides legal status: guidance, templates, drafts, presentations, and unknown-status material remain non-binding or unresolved even when their internal wording uses “must.” Conditions and exceptions are part of each restatement.
