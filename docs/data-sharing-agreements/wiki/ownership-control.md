> **DRAFT — NOT NATION-CONFIRMED.** This draft restates merged-catalog claims; no Nation-attributed claim is confirmed by the Nation. Human ratification remains required before presenting any entry as a Nation's requirement.

## Scope and range

This page scans the entire merged catalog (**64 records / 359 claims**) for data and records ownership and Tribal control. It includes **36** matching claims: **12** attached to Nation-linked instruments and **24** from intertribal, federal, state, academic, NGO, standards, or other issuers. Modal counts in this slice after applying authoritative correction `ev-director-0140`: `descriptive` 5; `may-permissive` 7; `must-shall` 23; `should-recommended` 1.

Selection rule: catalog category `ownership-control`. No uncataloged requirement statement has been added.

A modal never overrides legal status: guidance, templates, drafts, presentations, and unknown-status material remain non-binding or unresolved even when their internal wording uses “must.” Conditions and exceptions are part of each restatement.
