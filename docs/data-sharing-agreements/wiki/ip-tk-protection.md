# Intellectual property and Traditional Knowledge protection

This draft searches the entire merged catalog (64 records / 359 claims) and restates 10 claims across 6 records for this theme. It does not average different Nations' requirements or treat templates, guidance, drafts, scholarship, or unresolved instruments as enacted law.

## Range across instruments

- The enacted USGS directive permits a Tribe to request sensitive labeling for Indigenous Knowledge, cultural resources, and culturally significant site information; limits inclusion of Tribe-identified archaeological or sacred-site data subject to stated exceptions; and requires express written Tribal permission before interagency sharing of Tribe-owned data, including specified Indigenous Knowledge (`inst-018:c002`; `inst-018:c005`; `inst-018:c008`).
- Nation-authored materials in the catalog span different legal force and subject matter: Nez Perce materials state retained sovereignty over information and historical and cultural knowledge and prohibit covered guides from disclosing archaeological-site information; Spokane guidance addresses permitted effects on cultural resources and public conduct around artifacts (`nez-perce-2024-revised-research-permit-regulations-application:c021`; `nez-perce-revised-code-title-3:c011`; `preservation-program:c001`; `preservation-program:c002`; `preservation-program:c003`).
- The Klamath agreement record is `unknown-needs-review` and only describes cultural-resource confidentiality as an issue to address through consultation; it is not restated as a categorical nondisclosure rule (`klamath-special-use-permit:c004`).
- Draft THRO material recommends identifying cultural-stewardship protocols before collection when relevant. Its draft status and conditional scope are preserved (`inst-003:c008`).
- The cataloged claims in this theme center on cultural resources, sensitive sites, Indigenous Knowledge, stewardship, and retained authority. They do not establish a general patent, copyright, or trademark rule; non-discovery is not evidence of absence (`inst-018:c002`; `inst-018:c005`; `nez-perce-2024-revised-research-permit-regulations-application:c021`).
