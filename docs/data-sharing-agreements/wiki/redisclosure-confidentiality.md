# Redisclosure and Confidentiality

> **DRAFT — NOT NATION-CONFIRMED.** This draft restates merged-catalog claims; no Nation-attributed claim is confirmed by the Nation. Human ratification remains required before presenting any entry as a Nation's requirement.

## Scope and range

This page scans the entire merged catalog (**64 records / 359 claims**) for redisclosure limits and confidentiality duties. It includes **61** matching claims: **22** attached to Nation-linked instruments and **39** from intertribal, federal, state, academic, NGO, standards, or other issuers. Modal counts in this slice: `descriptive` 7; `may-permissive` 5; `must-shall` 42; `should-recommended` 7.

Selection rule: all `redisclosure-limits` claims, plus claims in other categories only when the cataloged claim or its conditions explicitly mention confidentiality, non-disclosure, or redisclosure. Category membership and legal force remain visible in every row.

A modal never overrides legal status: guidance, templates, drafts, presentations, and unknown-status material remain non-binding or unresolved even when their internal wording uses “must.” Conditions and exceptions are part of each restatement.
