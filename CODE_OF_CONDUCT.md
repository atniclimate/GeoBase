# Code of Conduct

GeoBase is built by and for Tribal Nations. We expect all participants to engage
with respect for Indigenous data sovereignty, communities, and one another.

## Our standards

- Act in ways that honor the CARE Principles for Indigenous Data Governance
  (Collective benefit, Authority to control, Responsibility, Ethics).
- Treat cultural knowledge and place-based data as belonging to the communities
  that steward it. Do not seek to extract, expose, or de-classify sovereign data.
- Be welcoming, patient, and constructive. Harassment or discrimination of any
  kind is not tolerated.

## Enforcement

Report concerns to the maintainers at climatechange@atnitribes.org. Maintainers
will review and respond as promptly as circumstances allow, and may remove
contributions or participants that violate these standards.
