## Dependency license audit

The full transitive license inventory of the cargo and pnpm dependency graphs
is generated by `scripts/license_audit.py` into `THIRD_PARTY_LICENSES.md` and
enforced by the `license-audit` CI job (`.github/workflows/license-audit.yml`).
The gate fails on any strong-copyleft (GPL/AGPL) dependency with no permissive
alternative in its SPDX expression — incompatible with the Apache-2.0
distributed binary. Weak file-level copyleft (MPL-2.0, and LGPL with a
permissive OR-branch) is allowed and reported.

As of 2026-07-16: **320 cargo packages, 0 disallowed** — the graph is
MIT/Apache-2.0-dominant; the only copyleft present is file-level MPL-2.0
(`cssparser`, `selectors`, `dtoa-short`, `option-ext`, and macros), which is
Apache-2.0-compatible for distribution. See `THIRD_PARTY_LICENSES.md` for the
exhaustive per-package table (regenerate with
`python scripts/license_audit.py --write THIRD_PARTY_LICENSES.md`).
